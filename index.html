Ganpat University

Ganpat University and Ganpat Vidyanagar, a collaborative initiative driven by philanthropic industrialists, technocrats, farmers, and businessmen, aim to uplift society through education. Established by the State Government in 2005 and approved by the UGC, the university has gained permanent membership in renowned academic and industry associations. It offers a wide range of industry-focused diploma, undergraduate, postgraduate, and research programs spanning fields like Engineering, Management, Pharmacy, Sciences, Architecture, Nursing, and more, aligning education with industry needs.

Navratri 2023

Navratri is a Hindu festival celebrating the triumph of good over evil, observed with nine nights of colorful dances, music, and devotion. During this time, devotees worship Goddess Durga and indulge in fasting and prayer.

Diwali 2023

Diwali, known as the Festival of Lights, is a Hindu celebration symbolizing the victory of light over darkness and good over evil. It's marked by dazzling lamps, colorful rangoli, and the exchange of sweets and gifts.
